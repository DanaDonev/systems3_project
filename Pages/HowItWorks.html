How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create an account as a pet owner or a pet sitter. Fill in your personal info and add your pet's profile or your experience details.

2. Browse Listings

Use filters like city, type of pet, and availability to find the perfect sitter for your pet.

3. Meet & Greet

Contact sitters and schedule a meeting to ensure it’s the right fit for both sides.

4. Make a Deal

Agree on the care period and pet needs. This deal acts as your digital agreement.

5. Pet Drop-off & Payment

Deliver your pet to the sitter and pay directly in person — no online payment needed.

6. Stay Connected

Use the mobile app to stay in touch and receive updates during the sitting.

7. Leave a Review

After the care period, rate the sitter and share your experience with others.

1. Join the Community

Register on the platform to access the forum. Only verified users can post or comment.

2. Ask a Question

Post questions related to pet health, behavior, or care. Add a photo if it helps explain your situation.

3. Choose Response Type

Decide if you want answers from everyone or only certified vets. Filter replies accordingly.

4. Engage in Discussion

Comment on other posts, offer help from your experience, or clarify your needs in your own thread.

5. Stay Notified

Receive notifications when someone replies to your post or when your question gets resolved.

Simple. Safe. Stress-free — for you and your pet.
